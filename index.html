OUR LADY'S WAILING HOUSE

BY “OUR LADY” we mean the Blessed Virgin Mary, mother of Jesus Christ and the mother of the church. To wail is to make a long loud high cry because one is sad or in pain. “Wailing house” is a house where such cry is made continuously.
